--- layout: post title: Get rid of pesky teenagers date: '2006-05-27T10:05:00.000-07:00' author: Richard Sprague tags: modified_time: '2006-05-27T10:13:18.210-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8203221.post-114874999819736775 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.richardsprague.com/2006/05/get-rid-of-pesky-teenagers.html --- Our hearing gets worse as we get older, especially our sensitivity to high-pitched sounds. UK entrepreneur Harry Stapleton turned that problem into a product, called Mosquito, that emits an annoying sound that only teenagers can hear. The rest of us can't hear a thing, so if you have a problem with pesky teenagers hanging out near you, just turn on the mosquito and watch them all run away.

You can buy it online for about $1000.
