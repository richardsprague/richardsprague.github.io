--- layout: post title: Anonymously access registration-required sites date: '2004-10-20T07:15:00.000-07:00' author: Richard Sprague tags: modified_time: '2004-10-20T07:15:14.523-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8203221.post-109828171452242979 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.richardsprague.com/2004/10/anonymously-access-registration.html --- BugMeNot.com is an idea whose time has come. If you need to view content on one of those sites (like NYTimes) that requires registration, type the URL into bugmenot instead. They enter a fake ID for you so you can view the content without telling the site who you are.

I don't mind registering at NYTimes or other places I use a lot, but sometimes I don't want to be bothered with entering fake info about myself just to view something once.
