--- layout: post title: WA State Election was a statistical tie date: '2005-01-11T06:49:00.000-08:00' author: Richard Sprague tags: modified_time: '2005-01-11T06:49:03.303-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8203221.post-110545494330483608 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.richardsprague.com/2005/01/wa-state-election-was-statistical-tie.html --- Seattle Times quotes several people saying "this election was 99.9% accurate", including Democratic Party spokeswoman Lisa Cohen, King county Elections Superintendent Bill Huennekens. But wait a minute! With more than 2M votes cast, 99.9% isn't good enough: that's still a margin of error of over 2,000 votes. With a 140-vote margin, the accuracy needs to be closer to 99.999% -- "5 nines", or roughly the standard used by the phone company.

This election was a statistical tie.
