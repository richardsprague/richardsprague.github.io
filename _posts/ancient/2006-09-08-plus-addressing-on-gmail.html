Here's a neat trick to give yourself an arbitrarily large number of email addresses.  On Gmail, just use whatever username you normally use, followed by the character '+' followed by anything else. For example:

myname+work@gmail.com gets delivered to myname@gmail.com

I'm going to use plus addressing whenever I fill out an online form so I can track how people get my email address.
