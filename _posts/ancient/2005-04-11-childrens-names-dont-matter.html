--- layout: post title: Children's names don't matter date: '2005-04-11T05:54:00.000-07:00' author: Richard Sprague tags: modified_time: '2005-04-11T05:54:54.893-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8203221.post-111322409489336629 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.richardsprague.com/2005/04/childrens-names-dont-matter.html --- A study of people's names by Steven D. Levitt and Stephen J. Dubner shows: "Today, more than 40 percent of the black girls born in California in a given year receive a name that not one of the roughly 100,000 baby white girls received that year. Even more remarkably, nearly 30 percent of the black girls are given a name that is unique among every baby, white and black, born that year in California."
