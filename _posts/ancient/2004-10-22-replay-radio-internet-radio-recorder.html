--- layout: post title: Replay Radio - Internet Radio Recorder date: '2004-10-22T09:38:00.000-07:00' author: Richard Sprague tags: modified_time: '2004-10-22T09:38:55.713-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8203221.post-109846313571319307 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.richardsprague.com/2004/10/replay-radio-internet-radio-recorder.html --- After knowing about it for months, I finally bought a copy of Replay Radio, basically a Tivo for Internet radio streams. It comes with a huge database of all the times/URLs for any radio program you'd want to record (from NRP to Rush Limbaugh).

I programmed it this morning to record Morning Edition for my commute to the office, and I have to say it was amazing. I was able to listen to a full hour of NPR in less than 15 minutes through a combination of skipping segments and using the double-speed playback option. Talk about efficient!
