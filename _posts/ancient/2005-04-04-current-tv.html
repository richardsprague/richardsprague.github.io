--- layout: post title: Current TV date: '2005-04-04T17:41:00.000-07:00' author: Richard Sprague tags: modified_time: '2005-04-04T17:41:38.706-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8203221.post-111266169870636293 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.richardsprague.com/2005/04/current-tv.html --- Current TV is Al Gore's new TV network, with an interesting twist. They are partnering with Google to include news updates based on real-time searches gleaned from the Google Zeitgeist. The idea is to direct your attention to news identified not by the creators of the news at the network, but by the live frequency of searches on Google.
