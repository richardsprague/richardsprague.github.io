--- layout: post title: School Levy Passed date: '2006-02-08T20:07:00.000-08:00' author: Richard Sprague tags: modified_time: '2006-02-08T20:07:30.876-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8203221.post-113945805066155878 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.richardsprague.com/2006/02/school-levy-passed.html --- The Mercer Island School District levy passed by a comfortable margin: more than 70%.

Homeowners like me are very supportive because we know that good schools translates into higher property values and ever-rising living standards.

About 5,000 people voted, out of a total voting population of 15,000. That's half as many who voted in last November's election. About 1,300 people on the Island can be officially classified as nay-sayers.
