--- layout: post title: Pepakura 3D paper printing date: '2005-08-06T15:13:00.000-07:00' author: Richard Sprague tags: modified_time: '2005-08-06T15:13:52.993-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8203221.post-112336643294064847 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.richardsprague.com/2005/08/pepakura-3d-paper-printing.html --- Kevin Kelly -- Cool Tools describes a shareware application from Japan called Pepakura (Paper Craft)


You enter 3D design information from any popular CAD application and it produces lines on paper that you can print and assemble. The makers of the software provide lots of samples on their web site including racecars, Godzilla figures, houses, and more. Anything you can model, you can build!
